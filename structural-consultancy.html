Retrofitting

What is retro fitting and how it work?

Retro fitting refer to modification and repair to structure of a building following it construction and occupation. Its increases the durability and perfomance of a structure. Retrofitting improves the strength and deficiency of concreate arising due to poor workmanship, design error and deterioration due to the aggression of harmfull alignments
