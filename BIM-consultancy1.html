What is BIM used for ?

Building INformation Modeling (BIM) is the foundation of digital transformation in the architecture,engineering,and construction(AEC) industry.

Based on an intelligent model and enabled by a cloud platform ,BIM integrates structured,multi-disciplinary data to produce a digital representation of an asset across its lifecycle,form planning and design to construction and operations.
